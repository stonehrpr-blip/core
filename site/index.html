FAQ

What can I improve with CORE?

Anything real — training, focus, money habits, sleep, doom-scrolling, junk food. It's built around proving real tasks and honest slip-logging, so it works for any goal or vice.

Is the AI coach real?

Yes — it reads your streak, slips and stats to respond in context, and you choose its tone. It knows what page you're on and remembers what you're working on.

How does proof work?

You upload a photo of the real task and the AI checks whether it counts. It can pass it, ask for better proof, or reject it — so progress on your card is always earned.

What if I break my streak?

Logging a slip honestly keeps your streak alive. For genuine off-days there's a weekly freeze, and a short restore window.

When does it launch?

CORE is in private beta now. Join the waitlist and you'll be first in line when spots open — early members get founder pricing.

Who can see my data?

You. CORE is private by default, we never sell your data, and social features are opt-in.
