CORE — Payment Flow Plan

Prepared for: Core · For: accountant / tax advisor / banker · Date:

Summary

CORE collects subscription revenue from two channels: Apple App Store (iOS app) and Stripe (web/PWA). Both channels deposit net revenue into the same Core bank account.

1 · iOS App Store Channel

User taps "Pay" in iOS app → Apple charges their Apple ID payment method → Apple holds funds ~30-45 days → Apple deducts commission (15% Y1, then 15-30%) → Apple wires remainder to Core bank account (configured at App Store Connect → Banking)

2 · Web Channel (Stripe)

User enters card on Stripe Checkout page → Stripe charges card → Stripe holds funds (7-day rolling for AU) → Stripe deducts 2.9% + 30¢ per charge → Stripe deposits remainder to Core bank (configured at Stripe Dashboard → Settings → Bank accounts)

4 · Setup Checklist (you must do)

  • 1 Register Core · obtain ABN
  • 2 Open a business bank account (NAB / CommBank / ANZ — separate from personal)
  • 3 Apple Developer Program — $99/yr · sign at developer.apple.com
  • 4 App Store Connect → Paid Apps Agreement · tax form (W-8BEN) · bank details
  • 5 Stripe account — verify identity · attach business bank account · enable AUD payout
  • 6 RevenueCat (optional) — unifies iOS + Stripe entitlements · free under $10K MTR
  • 7 GST registration once turnover exceeds $75K AUD/year (Australian threshold)

5 · Cancellation & Refund Policy

  • Trial: Cancellable any time before day 7. First charge only on day 7.
  • iOS: Cancellation via Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions (Apple-handled, immediate)
  • Web (Stripe): Cancellation via in-app Settings or Stripe customer portal. Refunds handled per Stripe policy.
  • Apple refund window: Up to 90 days, Apple makes the call. They deduct from your next payout if granted.
