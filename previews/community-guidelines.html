Community Guidelines

Last updated 2026-06-05
The short version
Core is a place for people quitting vape and building better habits. Be honest, be kind, and keep it safe. These guidelines apply anywhere you post, comment, share, or message inside Core — including your feed posts, Pact notes, share cards, and profile.

1. Who this is for

Core is for adults aged 18 and over. You must be 18+ to use Core or take part in its community. Content that targets, sexualises, or endangers minors is never allowed and will be removed and reported.

2. Be honest

Core is built on honest logging. In the community, that means:

  • Don't fake streaks, slips, or results to mislead others.
  • Don't post miracle claims or guarantees about quitting — share your own experience, not promises for everyone.
  • Don't impersonate another person, coach, or organisation.

3. Be kind

People here are doing hard things. Relapse is part of recovery. Do not:

  • Harass, bully, shame, or pile on anyone — including over a slip.
  • Post hate speech or slurs, or attack people based on who they are.
  • Threaten, intimidate, or reveal someone's private information (doxxing).

4. Keep it safe

To protect a community focused on quitting nicotine:

  • No promoting, selling, or glamorising vapes, nicotine, tobacco, or other addictive products.
  • No buying, selling, trading, or advertising any product, including in profiles and messages.
  • No content encouraging self-harm, suicide, eating disorders, or substance misuse.
  • No sexual content, nudity, graphic violence, or illegal content.
  • No spam, scams, phishing, malware, or off-platform link farming.

5. Crisis & safety

Core is a self-help tool, not a crisis service. If you or someone else is in danger or thinking about self-harm, contact local emergency services or a crisis line right away. In Australia: Lifeline 13 11 14, Beyond Blue 1300 22 4636. We may surface crisis resources when we detect content that suggests someone is at risk.

6. Your content

You own what you post, but you're responsible for it. Only share content you have the right to share, and remember that anything you post publicly may be seen by other members. Body Receipt photos and private logs are never shared unless you explicitly choose to.

7. Reporting

If you see something that breaks these guidelines, report it in-app or email corestudiosupport@gmail.com. Reports are confidential. We review reports and take action where appropriate.

8. Enforcement

When something breaks these guidelines, we may remove the content, issue a warning, limit features, or suspend or remove the account, depending on how serious or repeated the behaviour is. Serious cases — such as threats or content involving minors — may be removed immediately and reported to the relevant authorities. If you think we got it wrong, you can ask us to review the decision.

9. Changes

We may update these guidelines as the community grows. The current version always lives in the Core app. Using Core after changes take effect means you accept the updated guidelines.
