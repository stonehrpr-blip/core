FAQ
Can I switch plans later?
Yes — change monthly ↔ annual anytime from Settings → Subscription. Annual switchers get a pro-rata credit; monthly switchers start their year fresh.
What's in the trial?
Everything in Core Pro — unlocked for 7 days. We'll ask on day 6 if you want to keep going. Decline and you stay on Free.
What happens to my data if I cancel?
Stays yours. Habits, slips, streaks, and stats all remain — you just drop back to Free tier limits (1 habit, 1 stat visible). Export your data any time.
Refunds?
14 days on annual, no questions. Monthly is too cheap to refund — just cancel for the next month.
Why so cheap?
We want Core in front of millions, not thousands. Most habit apps charge $10–15/mo and gate the basics. We'd rather earn your year than your discount.
Core is local-first. Your data stays on your device unless you sync.
Subscription auto-renews. Cancel in Settings → Subscription any time.
