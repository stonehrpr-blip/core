How to rank up

1
Earn XP every day
Complete daily quests, hold your streak and finish boss quests — every action feeds your XP.
2

Each rank has three tiers — I, II, III. Fill the bar to advance a tier, then break into the next rank.
3
Unlock perks & cosmetics
Higher ranks unlock chest luck, frames, auras and exclusive card effects. Tap any rank to preview yours.
