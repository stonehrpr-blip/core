## Strategy

**The hook:** Most quit-vape content is sad/preachy. Ours is **visceral and gamified**. Show, don't tell.

**The 3 video archetypes** (rotate through these):

1. **Mechanic reveals** — "Watch what happens when I tap when I vape." Smoke pours out of phone, XP drops, lungs darken. People stop scrolling.
2. **Progress show-offs** — "Day 14. Watch my lungs heal back to pink." Time-lapse of the lung avatar healing.
3. **Coach screenshots** — Show the AI coach calling out a slip pattern. People love seeing AI being smart about them.

## 10 launch-week video scripts

Each script = 1 TikTok, 15–45 seconds, vertical 9:16.

### 1. "POV: you opened a vape-tracking app" — Mechanic reveal
- **Hook (0–2s):** Black screen. White text: "POV: you opened a vape-tracking app."
- **Beat 1 (2–5s):** Phone screen, dashboard showing Lungs 64/100 with the pink ring.
- **Beat 2 (5–10s):** Tap the "Mark a puff" button. SMOKE POURS OUT of the lungs avatar. "−25 XP" floats up.
- **Beat 3 (10–14s):** Lungs darken. Streak counter resets to 0. Recovery quest prompt slides up.
- **Outro (14–20s):** Voiceover: *"This is Core. It's brutal — and it's the only thing that's worked."*
- **CTA caption:** "Link in bio. I'm 14 days clean."

### 2. "Day 14 of letting an app punish me for vaping" — Day-in-life
- **Hook (0–2s):** Closeup of phone, lungs avatar slightly pink. "Day 14."
- **Beat 1 (2–8s):** "Every time I take a puff, I have to open this app and watch this happen." Tap. Smoke.
- **Beat 2 (8–14s):** Stat panel updates: -25 Lungs. Streak: 0. "First time I went a full week was 4 weeks ago."
- **Beat 3 (14–22s):** Pan to the trend graph going UP. "Now I have 14 days back-to-back."
- **CTA caption:** "It works because it's annoying to use. That's the whole point."

### 3. "The AI coach reads me to filth" — Coach reveal

- **Beat 3 (14–22s):** Show the next day. No slip. "And it worked."
- **CTA caption:** "It's like having a vape therapist in my pocket."

### 4. "Picking your habit on Core" — Onboarding tour
- **Hook (0–2s):** Welcome screen with floating orbs. "What if your habits were a video game?"

### 5. "My lungs in real-time" — Progress brag
