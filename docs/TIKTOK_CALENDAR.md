## Content principles

1. **Hook in first 1.5 seconds** — no intros, no "Welcome to my channel"
2. **Show the app, not your face** — TikTok rewards screen recordings of new apps
3. **Captions in-video** — sound off works for ~60% of TikTok viewers
4. **Single CTA per video** — "link in bio" appears once per video
5. **Reply to "what app is this" comments** within 1 hour for the first day
6. **Pin the best comment** — usually a satisfied user or a clarifying answer

## Track these metrics weekly

- Total followers (target: 5k by Day 30)
- Top video views (target: 1 video over 100k)
- Save rate per video (>5% = banger)
- "Link in bio" clicks (waitlist signups attributable)
- Comment-to-view ratio (>1% = engaged audience)
- Follower-to-watch-time graph (rising = algorithm loves you)

---

## Kill criteria (re-strategize if these hit)

- Followers <500 by Day 14 → mechanic isn't translating, try new hook
- Average views <500/video by Day 21 → algorithm shadow-banned, post slightly later/from different angle
- Save rate <2% → not adding value; need to switch to educational format
- 0 paid customers in launch week despite 50k+ views → conversion funnel broken, audit landing → waitlist → trial flow
