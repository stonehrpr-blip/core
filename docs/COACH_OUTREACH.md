# Coach outreach playbook

**Goal:** Onboard 5 friendly coaches in 30 days, then 10 paying by month 3.

## ICP definition (who to find)

Search LinkedIn Sales Navigator / Instagram / Twitter for:
- Job title contains: "addiction coach", "recovery coach", "sobriety coach", "smoking cessation coach", "vape coach", "habit coach", "behavior change specialist"
- Followers: 1k–50k (sweet spot — big enough to take seriously, small enough to reply to you)
- Posting frequency: at least 1 post/week (active)
- Niche overlap: any quit-substance specialist, especially vape/nicotine

**Tools:**
- Apollo or Hunter to enrich emails
- Phantombuster to scrape lists from #habitcoach, #quitvaping, #soberlife hashtags
- Calendly link for booking discovery calls (15 min, $0)

## Outreach cadence

| Touch | Day | Channel | What |
|-------|-----|---------|------|
| 1 | 0 | Instagram DM | First message (template A) |
| 2 | 3 | Instagram DM | Soft follow-up if no reply (template B) |
| 3 | 7 | LinkedIn DM | Different angle (template C) |
| 4 | 14 | Email | Long-form pitch (template D) |
| 5 | 30 | Mark as cold | Move on, retarget in 60 days |

50 first-touch DMs per week. Track in a spreadsheet. Expect 10–15% reply rate, 2–5% close rate to discovery call, 30% of calls → paid trial.

---

## Template A — Instagram DM (first touch · 3 lines max)

> Hey [first name] — saw your post about [specific thing they posted about, e.g., "the 3pm vape trigger"]. Massive nod.
>
> I built a tool that does exactly this for coaches — surfaces client triggers automatically + auto-generates session prep notes. Curious if you'd want a 15-min look (I'm gathering feedback from 5 coaches before we open).
>
> No pitch, no list. Just want to know what would actually help you.

**Why this works:**
- Specific reference proves you actually read their content
- Frames you as a builder asking for feedback, not selling
- Scarcity ("5 coaches") without being gross
- Explicit "no pitch" disarms

---

## Template B — Soft follow-up (Day 3, if no reply)

> Following up softly — totally fine if not your speed. If easier, here's a 2-min walk-through video: [Loom link]
>
> Either way, your content on [topic] is gold. Keep going.

---

## Template C — LinkedIn angle (Day 7)

> Hey [name] — I built a dashboard for addiction coaches that I want to put in front of 5 people whose work I respect.
>
> The pitch: live habit data from your clients' phones + AI-generated session prep notes. Saves about 30 min/session.
>
> If you can spare 15 minutes this week, I'll show you (and pay attention to your feedback, which is the point).
>
> Free month for the first 5 coaches who try it. Worth your time?

**Why different from A:** LinkedIn is more "professional / business" tone. Lead with the time-saver hook + reciprocity (free month).

## Template D — Long-form email (Day 14)

**Subject:** A vape coach dashboard built by someone who quit vaping

Hey [name],

Short note. I'm Stone — I built **Core**, an app for people quitting vape. It's been alive 30 days and I'm now building the coach side.

The B2C app does three things:
1. **Visceral feedback** — tap when you puff, watch smoke pour out of your lungs. Sounds harsh, but it kills the autopilot.
2. **AI pattern detection** — "You slip at 3pm. Here's what to try."
3. **Recovery quests** — 60-second box-breath after a slip, claw back XP.

The coach dashboard I'm building lets you see ALL your clients' habit data live, with AI-generated session prep notes that take ~30 minutes off your prep time.

Three things would help me right now:
- 15 min to show it to you (Calendly: [link])
- Brutal feedback on what's missing
- If it earns it, one client trial

I'd love yours. Free for 90 days for the first 5 coaches.

Stone

## Reply playbook

> Coach Pro is $399/mo (5 client seats included). Coach Studio $799 (15 seats). Free for 90 days for the first 5 founding coaches. Want a 15-min demo this week?

**Reply: "Send me more info"**
> Sent a quick Loom: [link]. Or 15 min on Calendly if you want me to walk you through: [link].

**Reply: "I use Notion / Practice / SimplePractice"**
> Totally — those don't pull live habit data from your client's phone though. The AI session prep is the unlock. 15 min and you'll see if it's worth switching off your stack: [link]

**Reply: "Not interested"**
> Appreciate the reply. Mind if I check back in 6 months? Quitting is having a moment and I'd rather earn your business slowly than burn the bridge.

**Reply: "I'm not a coach yet, I'm doing it for free"**
> Even better — Core for Coaches is free below 3 clients. Want early access? [link]

## What to NOT say (kills replies)

- ❌ "Hope you're doing well!"
- ❌ "I came across your profile"
- ❌ "Quick question"
- ❌ "Just 5 minutes of your time"
- ❌ Any cold-template-detector trigger phrases
